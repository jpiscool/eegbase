## Left column — The opportunity

### The gap
Mendi sells exceptional consumer hardware. Clinicians can't currently prescribe it because there's no clinical software layer. EEGBase is that layer.

### What clinicians do with it
- Pair Mendi with a HIPAA video call — see the at-home client's live prefrontal signals during a telehealth session
- Auto-correlate Mendi data with Apple Health · Oura · mood diary · medication adherence to surface causal-looking drivers
- Generate SOAP / DAP / BIRP / GIRP / PIE / SIRP notes from session audio
- Submit CMS-1500 claims for reimbursable codes (90901 / 90875 / 90876)
- Export every session as BIDS-fNIRS for the research registry

### The numbers (illustrative · multi-clinic registry, n=2,840 ADHD adolescent)
- 74% reached clinically significant improvement within 22 sessions (n=847 matched-profile cohort)
- Mean ΔHbO +12.3% over a 20-session protocol (registry-wide)
- ADHD-RS −8.1 points, d = 0.62, p < 0.001 (n=2,840)
